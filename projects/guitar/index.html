** ABOUT ** This collection do contain 100 tracks of the best guitar music in the world, played by those amongst the greatest guitarists. The works are certainly great:- some of them well-known and others completely unknown. I love the simple layout of this collection - CD 1 Baroque and before, CD 2 Spanish Guitar Vol 1, CD3 Spanish Guitar Vol 2, CD 4 Rodrigo Conciertos, CD 5 Latin American Guitar, CD 6 The Modern Guitar. I am convinced that you will find many tracks that you will enjoy. Among my favourites are an arrangement of a Bach Harpsichord Concerto on CD 1 played by Christopher Parkening, Rodrigo 3 Spanish Pieces played by Manuel Barrueco on CD3, the whole of CD 4, and Beatles arrangements on CD 6. Every time I listen my enjoyment grows as I find something new. ** FEATURES ** -World's greatest masterpieces -Listen Anywhere without Internet -Background playing(both Home Screen and Lock Screen) -Playback Modes:Loop - Shuffle - Repeat -Compatible for all iPod, iPhone and iPad -iPhone 5 Retina Support -Timing and shake to switch choice ** THE COLLECTION AND COMPOSERS ** CD 1: Baroque And Before  CD 2: Spanish Guitar Vol.1  CD 3: Spanish Guitar Vol.2  CD 4: Rodrigo  CD 5: Latin American Guitar  CD 6: The Modern Guitar J.
